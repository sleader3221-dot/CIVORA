# Demo Video Script

## 0:00-0:15 - Problem

Fraud and incident teams receive thousands of suspicious events, but the real bottleneck is proving which events matter and preserving evidence for action.

## 0:15-0:35 - Solution

ProofPilot AI turns suspicious events into risk-scored cases, linked entity timelines, tamper-evident ledgers, and compliance-ready reports.

## 0:35-1:25 - Working App Demo

Open the landing page and click Launch Live Demo. In Judge Demo Mode, generate 1,000 suspicious events for Northstar Pay. Open the latest critical case. Show the risk score, reason codes, timeline, entity graph, evidence rows, and ledger chain. Click Verify Evidence Chain. Click Generate Report and show the printable report preview.

## 1:25-2:05 - AWS Database Architecture

Open Architecture. Explain that DynamoDB stores the raw high-volume event stream with organization, entity, and severity access patterns. Aurora DSQL stores the relational workflow system of record: cases, risk scores, notes, evidence ledger, reports, users, API keys, and billing plans.

## 2:05-2:35 - Business Impact

ProofPilot is monetizable as a B2B SaaS: Starter at $29/month for 10k events, Growth at $99/month for 100k events, and Enterprise for SSO, retention, and compliance exports.

## 2:35-2:55 - Closing

Close on the system health page showing demo or live AWS mode, the environment readiness checklist, and the H0 submission phrase with #H0Hackathon.
